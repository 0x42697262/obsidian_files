# Cyber Threat

---

Anything that has the **potential** to cause serious harm to any digital information systems.

When manifested, it becomes a [[Cyberattack]].

## Common Cyber Threats

- [[Distributed Denial of Service]]
- [[Malware]]
- [[Social Engineering]]
- [[Supply Chain Attack]]
- Data Breaches
- Insider Threats
