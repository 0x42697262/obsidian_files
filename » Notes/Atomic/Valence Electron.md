## What is a Valence Electron

Valence Electrons are *s* and *p* [[Electron|electrons]] located at the outermost [[Valence Shell|shell]] of an [[Atom|atom]].

- max number of [[Electron|electrons]] in an [[Atom|atom]] is 8
