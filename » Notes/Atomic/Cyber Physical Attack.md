A [[Cyberattack]] that involves the physical environment.

Examples:

- Malicious USB Cable
- Malicious flash drives such as [[USB Rubber Ducky]] and [[USB Killer]]
- [[Card Cloning]]
- [[Skimming]]

Relates to [[Asset Security]] [[Security Domains (CISSP)]].
